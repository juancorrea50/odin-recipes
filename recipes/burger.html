Hamburger

Yum! Boiga(Burger)!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Ground beef or equivalent
  • Slice of Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • (Optional) Condiments
  • (Optional) Butter

Directions

  1. Flatten ground beef into patties
  2. Salt and pepper the patties
  3. Cook patties until desired finish
  4. Add cheese to patties while brown cooked
  5. Butter the buns!
  6. Place buns on grill surface to toast
  7. Once finished, place cooked patties onto toasted buns
  8. Add vegetables
  9. Add Condiments
  10. Enjoy!
