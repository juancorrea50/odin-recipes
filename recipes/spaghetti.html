Spaghetti

Yum! Spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Meat of your choice!

Directions

  1. Cook pasta for 10-12 minutes
  2. Halfway through the timer, heat up pan for the meat
  3. Salt, pepper, and cook meat for 5-6 minutes or until cooked
  4. Strain Pasta once timer is up
  5. Pour sauce into cooked meat
  6. Mix with meat and dump mixture into the strained pot of pasta
  7. Enjoy!
